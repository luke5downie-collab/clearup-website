What ClearUp is.

ClearUp is a tool for tracking acne over time. You take daily photos and log changes, and our AI (Claude by Anthropic) reads your text logs to spot patterns.

What ClearUp is NOT.

ClearUp is not a medical device. ClearUp does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The AI feedback is informational only — based on your own logs — and is not a substitute for advice from a dermatologist or doctor. If you're experiencing severe acne, scarring, or any skin concern that worries you, please see a doctor.

Your account.

There is no account. The app works locally on your device.
